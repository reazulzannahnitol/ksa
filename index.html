About Us

Welcome to Navon Jobs KSA, a leader in the recruitment industry, specializing in connecting skilled and unskilled blue-collar laborers with employment opportunities in Saudi Arabia and beyond. As part of the global Navon Jobs network, we are committed to bridging the gap between employers and job seekers, ensuring reliable, efficient, and ethical recruitment solutions.

Navon Jobs is a global recruitment company, operating in Romania, UAE, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Nepal, and KSA, with our financial headquarters in Delaware, USA. We have extensive experience in facilitating job placements for laborers across multiple sectors, including construction, manufacturing, logistics, and more.

From initial job matching to visa assistance and post-placement services, we ensure that both employers and candidates experience a seamless journey. Our tailored approach enables us to identify candidates who meet specific job requirements, while also prioritizing workers’ welfare and job satisfaction. By partnering with trusted companies and offering roles that include competitive pay, safe working conditions, and career growth opportunities, Navon Jobs KSA is dedicated to uplifting individuals and contributing to economic development across the Middle East and beyond.

Our Saudi Arabian (KSA) Branch Specializes In Two Key Areas:

1. Bringing Blue-Collar Workers to KSA

We source and recruit skilled and unskilled labor from across Asia and Africa, ensuring companies in KSA have access to a steady stream of qualified workers for their industries. Our rigorous selection process ensures that only the most capable candidates are matched with our clients’ needs.

2. Sending KSA Workers to Europe

We offer Saudi laborers the opportunity to work in various European countries, including Romania, Croatia, Hungary, and more. We handle the entire process, from visa applications to job placement, ensuring a seamless transition for workers looking to advance their careers abroad.

Emilia Foundation

Our Story

We strongly believe that giving enriches you, and anyone can offer a smile, a kind word, love, compassion, or understanding – a piece of your heart! Every day we receive gifts that we should appreciate: a new day of life, a new sunrise, the clear blue sky, the beautiful nature that delights our eyes and lifts our spirits, and the pure smile of a child. Let us give, by loving, and love, by giving! Only in this way will we understand the good that a simple smile can bring.

If you want to advance on the path of knowledge, gift a book; if you want to have an enlightened heart, offer comfort to those in need; if you want to know freedom, buy a dove from the market and set it free to fly towards the sky.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring prosperity to families and the Neamț community, to support young people in discovering their vocations through educational, cultural, and artistic programs, and to help those in need lead a decent life, because the well-being of each individual depends on the common good.

Our Vision

In our vision, every person can contribute to a better world with a smile or a kind word. We believe that only through mutual support can we have a united community based on healthy values, and only then can we live in peace and harmony.

Our Values

Our values: generosity, respect, involvement, assistance, Christianity, altruism, gratitude, generosity.

Our Goal

Our goal is to initiate social programs and cultural and educational events to create a favorable environment for the development of children and families from disadvantaged backgrounds.

The Emilia Foundation operates in the localities of Neamț County, which has over 446,000 inhabitants. Many families in the county live on the edge of existence, children have difficult access to education and other extracurricular school programs.
